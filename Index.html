About Our Abaca Bags

Product Name: Iloilo Heritage Abaca Tote & Crossbody Collection

Description: Each bag is handwoven from premium abaca fiber – a renewable resource native to the Philippines. We combine traditional weaving techniques with modern design to create pieces that suit everyday use and special occasions. Bags feature reinforced handles, water-resistant linings, and hand-carved wooden accents.
